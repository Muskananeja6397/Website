Manali

Manali is located in the heart of Himachal Pradesh. According to Hindu mythology, the town's name is derived from "Manu-Alaya," which means "the abode of Manu," the first man and the lawgiver. Legend has it that sage Manu stepped off his ark here to recreate human life after a great flood had deluged the world. Manali is often referred to as the "Valley of the Gods" and is nestled in the majestic Himalayas, showcasing the unparalleled creativity of nature.
