Top Places to Visit in Kedarnath

Kedarnath Temple

One of the holiest temples in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and a must-visit for pilgrims.

Chorabari Lake

A scenic lake known for its pristine beauty and serene environment, perfect for trekking enthusiasts.

Gaurikund

The starting point of the Kedarnath trek, famous for its hot springs and scenic views.
